Sport Overview

Associaton football is an invasion sport, commonaly called football or soccer depending on the country. It is played on a field approximantley 105m x 68m, with 11 players on the pitch for each team.

Positions

There are 4 main position groups for the 11 players on a football pitch: goalkeeper, defense, midfeild, and forward. Each team has 1 goalkeeper on the field at a time, they wear a seperate color from the rest of their team and are allowed to use their hands within the goalkeeping box. Teams will typically have between 3-5 defenders on the field at a time. Their main job is to prevent the oppossing team from scoring. Midfielder are the most versitile position, and have multiple subclasses including cetral, attacking, defensive, and left/right wing. They are generally involved in both attcking and defending. Forwards main goal is to score goals for their team. Depending on the lineup wings can be classified as midfielders or forwards.
